Sometimes, Earth can be a depressing planet. It seems today that most news is bad news, or even just news we don't really want to hear about. We here at the Daily Planets are not advocates of distraction from current or past events. However, we find it unwise to let the bleak nature of this world consume you. Instead, why not go a third way? If life on this planet seems depressing, why not supplement the days headlines with imagery from a less terrible, more liveable nearby planet? Introducing the Daily Planets, Earths first multi-planetary independent media source. (Possibly. We lack enough evidence to confidently claim this title, sadly. We need more funding for that.)
